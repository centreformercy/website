A Place to Pray, Reflect, or Meet

We want to share our gift with you!

For the past 20 years, we (Fr. Ed and Fr. Rosy) have made our cabin in Wright City available for retreats, time with the family and even some vacations and honeymoons. Taking care of our 15 acres became more of a chore so we sold that place in the fall of 2004. But all is not lost. Fr. Pat Malone (classmate from Wichita) has joined us in purchasing a beautiful home (eventually, our retirement home) in Innsbrook Estates, 5 miles south of Wright City, on #F Highway on Lot # 1181.

You may share in our Gift!
