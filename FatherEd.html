Remembering Father Ed

"We are very excited that all of you who are seeing this website have the opportunity to experience a little of
Fr. Ed Schramm.

Fr. Ed was a wonderful Priest, person and friend. He wanted all of us to be filled with the peace and love of Jesus Christ. We remember so well the times we spent with him. In any decision, he always stopped to pray first. When we were struggling with anything in our life, he would pray with us.
His life was filled with Jesus, the Church, and the people he served. We know Fr. Ed because of our ministry in Worldwide Marriage Encounter, which is a ministry of relationships. He was dedicated to furthering the work of our ministry to bring love and joy to marriages and to the relationships of priests with the people they serve. He helped us to keep the Holy Spirit present in our ministry and to always keep God in the center of our work.
To learn more about our ministry, please visit www.wwme.org 
Fr. Ed, we miss you, we love you, and we are excited that you are an angel in heaven watching over us. "
Lee & Jan Kremer
