Matthew 6:21

For where your treasure is, there also will your heart be.

Psalm 26:2

Examine me, Lord, and test me; search my heart and mind.

Matthew 5:8

Blessed are the clean of heart, for they will see God.

Proverbs 23:26

My son, give me your heart,and let your eyes keep to my ways.

Psalm 51:12

A clean heart create for me, God; renew within me a steadfast spirit.

Psalm 73:26

Though my flesh and my heart fail, God is the rock of my heart, my portion forever.

Philippians 4:7

Then the peace of God that surpasses all understanding will guard your hearts and minds in Christ Jesus.

Psalm 119:2

Blessed those who keep his testimonies, who seek him with all their heart.

Psalm 37:4

Find your delight in the LORD who will give you your hearts desire.

God is wise in heart and mighty in strength; who has withstood him and remained whole?

Psalm 9:1

I will praise you, LORD, with all my heart; I will declare all your wondrous deeds.

Proverbs 3:5

Trust in the LORD with all your heart, on your own intelligence do not rely.

Psalm 34:19

The LORD is close to the broken hearted, saves those whose spirit is crushed.

Proverbs 4:23

With all vigilance guard your heart, for in it are the sources of life.

They had not understood the incident of the loaves. On the contrary, their hearts were hardened.

Psalm 24:4

The clean of hand and pure of heart, who has not given his soul to useless things, what is vain.

Romans 12:2

Do not conform yourselves to this age but be transformed by the renewal of your mind, that you may discern what is the will of God, what is good and pleasing and perfect.

Psalm 19:15

Let the words of my mouth be acceptable, the thoughts of my heart before you, LORD, my rock and my redeemer.
