In Sinu JESU, JESUS gave several messages to the Monk at Knock about the healing of the Church through her priests and bishops as they come to Knock.

"Mankind will not have peace until it turns to the Fount of My Mercy." (Diary 699)

I believe that we are living in a time of revelation as the mysteries of GOD are being revealed ~ Our LORD is calling us to come to Him more often than in the past ~ in the Eucharist and in Adoration as we also repent, confess, and ask forgiveness of our sins and of others and live His Mercy.

In returning from Knock and Ireland, there is an urgency to live our faith! Many individuals are listening and hearing from Our LORD and Our Lady and a "movement of GOD's Light" is streaming from the Emerald Isle! 

It is thought that GOD was 'pulling back the veil" between Heaven and earth by this 'visitation' of St. Joseph, Our Blessed Mother, St. John, the Evangelist, the 'LAMB of GOD' standing before the Cross on the altar, surrounded by Heavenly Angels". This visitation took place outside of the Church of Saint John the Baptist on its back wall. Some believe that this is the Heavenly Banquet to which we are invited to share and participate at every Eucharist!

There were many significant "moments" of our pilgrimage, which I will be sharing with you soon! JESUS, 'the Lamb of GOD, Who takes away the sins of the world' and Our Blessed Mother are calling us ~ knocking on the door of our lives and our heart to enter especially more deeply into the Mystery of the Eucharist ~ the Heavenly Banquet. They are inviting us to share in the Glorious Presence of the Blessed Trinity in the Eternal Banquet of the Heavenly realm, receiving Our LORD's Body and Blood so as to live our faith with the graces received from the Eucharist ~ loving GOD, neighbor and ourselves, and committing to a sacrificial offering of coming before Him in Eucharistic Adoration.

He urgently begs and desires to sustain and strengthen us at this critical and dark time! One of the women we met said that JESUS desires to speak to Each of Us ~ JESUS waits for us ~ He desires us to come to Him, resting in Him so that He can strengthen and hold us, heal us and speak to Our Hearts! JESUS said, "Silence is the deepest language of Our LOVE. A soul just needs to come to Me. I long to speak to everyone's heart and to reassure them of My ETERNAL and NEVER-ENDING LOVE"!

Like the Apparition at Knock 139 years ago before the Cross, "The LAMB of GOD, waits on the altar S I L E N T L Y for us!
