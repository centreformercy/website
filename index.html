About Us

"May Your Mercy, O Lord,
be upon us;
we have put our trust in You."
Psalm 33:22

Welcome ~ Thank you for visiting us as we are in the midst of creating this web ~

Here we'd like to begin to share with you Our Love of the FATHER, JESUS and Our Beloved Mother Mary,
the HOLY SPIRIT
and Saint Joseph and the Saints,
whose constant intercession upon which we rely for help.

We would also like to share about our dear Priest Friend,
Fr. Edward J Schramm,
who died on Divine Mercy Sunday,
March 30, 2008.  
You will know him as a gentle shepherd,
whose heart belonged to GOD,
Our Blessed Mother and His flocks,
his family, friends and parishioners.  Among many others,
you may be included,
the Marriage Encounter Communities and
the Focolare Movement, continued to bring Him GOD's love, comfort and peace in his most difficult days of illness and his last weeks of suffering.
His last requests were:
"to see His Daddy (GOD) face to face."

All of the Saints, especially our Youth, are waiting to be known and asked for Intercession.
Heaven's Love knows no boundaries.

"Give thanks to the Lord,
for He is good,
    His mercy endures forever."
Psalm 118:1
